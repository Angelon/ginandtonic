Tim McCracken's vision to create a fully charged digital design agency specializing in online visual entertainment was born in 2008 as Gin and Tonic. Located in Chapel Hill, North Carolina servicing clients throughout North America and the UK.

In today’s digital era, technology constantly evolves whilst the market continually adapts. At Gin and Tonic we embrace this rapid metamorphose and are in the vanguard of the latest trends enabling us to inspire our clients.

Today, Tim leads an interactive team of vibrant and talented designers, programmers, producers and animators, whose creative abilities and experiences, aligned with today’s current trends, are designed to achieve success and gain a distinctive edge in today’s marketplace. Our Team can build the vital tools to successfully reach your target audience through brand recognition and social media, to showcase the impact of digital advertising, from innovative websites, mobile and tablet apps, video production and interactive installations.
